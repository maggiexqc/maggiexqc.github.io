Moody

Written in: Javascript, Python

Technologies used: React Native, OpenAI API, HealthKit API

Info:

Moody is a personal wellness and fitness app powered by AI. Users can chat to Moody's AI chatbot and receive dymanic personalized advice and tips on how to improve their mood and health based on their real-time metrics from Apple Health. Users can also scroll through articles and videos suggested by AI for their physical and mental needs.

This app started as a 24-hour hackathon project with a team of 4, completing its core fundamentals into a well drafted app. Over time, I began to come up with new ideas and design aspects to add to the app where I have been working on upon graduating.

Currently in development, new features and display coming soon!
