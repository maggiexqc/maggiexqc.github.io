Protein Analysis App

Written in: Python

Libraries used: NumPy, Pandas

Info:

This app takes in high-throughput protein data for bioinformatic analysis for evaluation in the form of visual plots of gene expression levels between COVID-19 patients with olfactory loss (loss of smell) and non infected individuals.

This is a research project belonging to Gong Lab at UC Davis School of Medicine and has the potential to identify quantitative and qualitative protein changes associated with COVID-19-induced olfactory loss and to better understand viral infection induced responses in olfactory systems.

Fun fact: I started off as the research assistant to extract the proteins from samples and ended up becoming the co-developer for this project.

Presenting the research:

In April 2024 I presented this research at the UC Davis Undergraduate Research Conference!

Contents of this application belongs to Gong Lab and is unable to be shown.
